👨‍🏫 My Experience as a teacher

Teaching has always been one of my passions, and over the years, I have had the opportunity to share my knowledge and experience with students of different levels. Below, I'll tell you a bit more about my journey as a teacher:

📚 Private Lessons

I have worked giving private lessons to high school, college, and university students in science subjects. From mathematics to physics and programming, I have focused on helping my students understand complex concepts in a clear and comprehensible way, adapting my teaching to their needs and pace.

💻 Training at OpenWebinars

In addition to traditional teaching, I had the honor of being part of OpenWebinars, where I taught 4 courses and workshops focused on key topics in the software world and test automation. These are the courses I have offered:

  • Automating UI Tests in Angular with Protractor
    In this course, I taught how to automate user interface tests in Angular applications using Protractor. If you're interested, you can watch the full course on this 📺 YouTube link.
  • Creating Pipelines with GitLab
    In this workshop, participants learned how to create and manage automated pipelines with GitLab, optimizing their continuous integration and deployment (CI/CD) processes in software projects.
  • Automated Testing with Docker
    This course focused on using Docker to create isolated and reproducible test environments, enabling more efficient and consistent automated testing.
  • Testing APIs with Postman
    In this workshop, I taught how to perform API tests using Postman, a key tool for developers who want to verify the behavior and functionality of APIs.

Each of these courses has a practical focus, providing participants with the tools needed to apply what they've learned immediately to their projects.
